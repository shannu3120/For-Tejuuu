💭 Nuvvu Cheppina Matalu kii... Nenu Cheppalekapoyina Matalu

Last konni rojullo nuvvu aadina konni matalu naku chala nachchayi amma ❤️

Modatidi...

"First job thechukundham... nuvvu, nenu, Vishnu, Srujuna andaram kalisi car lo trips ki vellipovachu... enjoy cheyyachu..." ani nuvvu cheppinappudu,

Nuvvu aa kshanam entha santoshamga feel ayyavo naku teliyadhu gani, nenu matram danikanna enno retlu ekkuva happy ga feel ayyanu.

Aa maata vinnappati nunchi aa scene naa mind lo chala sarlu ochindi. Oka roju aa kala nijam avvali ani manaspoorthiga korukunna.

Tharvata...

Maa annani "bava garu" ani pilichinappudu naa anandhaniki hadhulu levu. Kani adhi kuda oka pakka ayithe...

"Nee vadinani nenu cheppakundane akka ani pilustha" ani nuvvu annappudu matram nijanga flat ayipoya.

Aa chinna maatalu neeku casual ga anipinchayi emo...

Kani naku matram chala special ga anipinchayi.

Kani ivanni pakkana pedithe, nenu cheppalanukunna oka maata undi.

Nuvvu eppudo oka sari...

" Nen ninnu line lo petukogalanu " ani annav.

A maata vinnappudu nenu navvina...

Kani manasulo matram okate anipinchindi...

" Nannu nee line lo petesukora please raa...

Kavali antey nee kaallu mokkutha raa...

Jeevithantham nethoney untanu... "

Endukante nee tho unna prati conversation, prati smile, prati chinna memory naku chala viluvainavi.

Inko maata...

Gurthu petuko.

Naa venta adugu vesina prati sari nenu untanu.

Ee roju...
Repu...
Leda eppudaina...

Maa family gurinchi matladinappudu kuda nenu cheppalanukunna oka maata undi.

Maa illu nee illu.

Nenu unna, lekapoyina...

Maa inti thalupulu neekosam eppudu teriche untayi.

Nuvvu aa intiki ye roopam lo vastavo adhi nee ishtam.

Kodalu ga vachina...
Snehithuraliga vachina...
Yela vachina...

Nuvvu maa intiki vachina prathi sari, maa vallaki nuvvu kuthure.

Adhi nenu unna lekapoyina maaradu.

Maa vallaki nuvvu eppatiki special ye.

Ivanni nenu appudu cheppalekapoyina matalu.

Nuvvu cheppina konni matalaki, nenu manasulo ichukonna konni samadhanalu. ❤️
